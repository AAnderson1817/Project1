Snowden Simulator

The Truth is Out There

Instructions

You are a prospective whistleblower working for the NSA, and an acolyte of Edward Snowden. Devoted, you've taken it upon yourself to unveil the dirty secrets of domestic surveilance. Using guile, cunning, and quick fingers, you are tasked with leaking as much accurate information to The Guardian as possible in the time allotted. First to 10 wins!
